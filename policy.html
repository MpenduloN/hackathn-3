About Policy

This is a page dedicated to policy information.

Policies are essential for guiding decision-making and ensuring that actions are aligned with organizational goals.

Provision of Land and Assistance Act (1993): Facilitates land designation and settlement for land reform.

Restitution of Land Rights Act (1994): Provides for the restitution of land rights to those dispossessed after 1913.

Extension of Security of Tenure Act (1997): Protects the rights of people living on communal land.

Land Reform (Labour Tenants) Act (1996): Aims to protect the rights of labour tenants.

Communal Property Associations Act (1996): Regulates the formation and management of communal property associations.

National Environmental Management Act (1998): Provides a framework for environmental management and protection.

National Water Act (1998): Governs the use and management of water resources.

National Forests Act (1998): Regulates the management and conservation of forests.

Spatial Planning and Land Use Management Act (2013): Provides for spatial planning and land use management.

AgriBEE Charter (2008): Promotes black economic empowerment in the agricultural sector.

National Development Plan (2012): Aims to eliminate poverty and reduce inequality by 2030.

These policies collectively aim to address historical injustices, promote sustainable land use, and support rural development in South Africa.
